Description

One of just 518 GT500KR convertibles built in 1968, this black beauty is powered by a factory 428ci Cobra Jet V8 with a 3-speed automatic and Traction-Lok rear. Features include a power-operated white soft top, Shelby 10-spoke wheels, front disc brakes, custom interior trim with Carroll Shelby’s signature, and a roll bar with inertia seatbelts.
